1. Numerical data - Arithmetic operation¶

  • Arithmetic Operation: Covers standard operations like Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication (including Basic and Dot Product), Division (Floating point and Integer point), Modulo, and Power. These operations also allow for filling missing values, selecting the axis of operation, and working with multi-index levels.
  • Reverse Arithmetic Operation: Lists the reverse counterparts of these standard operations, specifically reverse add, reverse subtraction, reverse multiplication, reverse division, reverse modulo, and reverse power.
